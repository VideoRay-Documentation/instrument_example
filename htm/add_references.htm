How to Add References to your Project

This step allows your project to access the Instrument libraries provided by VideoRay

  1. In the "Solution Explorer" right click on "References"
  2. Select "Add Reference…"
  3. Select "Assemblies"
  4. Click "Extensions"
  5. Scroll all the way down the middle and select "vrLib" & "vrLibUI"
  6. Click "OK"
If "vrLib" & "vrLibUI" are not located under extensions:
  1. Click "Browse…" At the bottom of the window
  2. Navigate to where your "InstrumentHostSample" folder is and go into "Bin/Debug"
  3. Select "vrLib.dll" & "vrLibUI.dll"
