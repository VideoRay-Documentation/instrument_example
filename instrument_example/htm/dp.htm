Development Procedures and Overview

The techniques and procedures shown in the document are just a sampling of one potential way to make an instrument. A simple outline to follow while creating your instrument is:

  • Determine what you want your instrument to do
  • Determine which parameters you must alter to get your desired result. Click here for the vrLib documentation.
  • Download the sample solution here if you haven't already
  • Create a new project within the sample solution(starting here)
  • Correctly sync your new project to the sample solution(starting here)
  • Copy necessary bits of code from prior projects into your code
  • Code your instrument
  • Test and refine your instrument in the Visual Studio
  • Integrate your instrument into Cockpit
